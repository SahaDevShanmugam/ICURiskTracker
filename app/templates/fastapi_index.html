Data analysis

The deterioration model ingests rolling hemodynamic statistics (MAP / HR / RR trends, variability, and hypotension burden) aligned to your training configuration, then outputs a calibrated probability mapped to a 0–100 score with tier cutoffs from risk_display.tiers in config.yaml. Use the cohort seed and model selector above to stress different synthetic patients; each bay replays a full simulated stay on a loop so you can watch risk evolve as vitals stream.
